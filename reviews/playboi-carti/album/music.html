I Am Music

Playboi Carti
4/10
Released on Mar 17, 2025 | Reviewed on Mar 24, 2025

Carti’s Back… But This Ain’t It

Carti's long awaited third album MUSIC is finally out! This joint dropped on March 17th, 2025, a whole 5 years since his last masterpiece Whole Lotta Red. Spanning 18 tracks and clocking in at just about 60 minutes, MUSIC is a mess to say the least, especially for a 5 YEAR project. Carti has Kendrick Lamar on the track Good Credit, but even though he drops a sold verse, You can tell he is holdin back to match Carti's inconsistent flow, as if he didn't want to outshine him too hard. Comparing this project to his previous one, this feels like Carti just threw stuff together with no direction at all, despite how long it's runtime is.

The beats are all over the place, and not in a good way either. Some tracks sound like they were slapped together in a rush, with Carti’s mumbled delivery feeling near effortless in the worst way possible. It's like he forgot what made his earlier stuff so hard. Even the hyped up moments fall flat, and by the end, you just sit there wondering what the point of waiting a solid 5 years was even for. MUSIC is a straight up letdown, a basic ass followup to all of the hype and anticipation Carti had built up over these years. There are a couple decent tracks like Evil Jordan, but overall, This is not what I was hoping for. This seems to be a fan favorite rather than a universal banger. But I did expect that to come after the single All Red dropped, which in itself, was not a good single, and it didn't even make it to MUSIC!

Rating

With all said, MUSIC gets a 4 on the Doutree Richter Scale. The lack of consistency in each of the tracks combined with Carti's terrible flow and lack of direction all leads to an album that fails to shake up the floor. Some tracks you can feel but most tracks on this album do not deliver enough energy for me to rate it higher on the scale, which for a 5 year project, is not a good thing.

Favorite Track: Evil Jordan

Least Favorite Track: Good Credit

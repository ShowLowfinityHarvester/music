I Am Music

Playboi Carti
4/10
Mar 17, 2025

Carti's Back!!

It has been 5 years since Carti's last album. No more teasing, this project is finally out!

I took a listen to the album on sunday, and it was interesting, to say the least. The collaboration with Kendrick Lamar on tracks like Good Credit, Kendrick delivers an impressive verse but seems to hold back, possibly to match Carti's inconsistent flow. The album makes Carti’s previous work seem like it truly had a point, while this one lack of direction despite how long the runtime is. Overall, This is a very disappointing follow-up to the anticipation built over the years years, with its worst moments sounding near effortless and pretty thrown together."

For a 5 year project... This ain't good

This album was worked on for the past 5 years, but somehow, it feels sloppy and rushed, not what we were hoping for.

The album's commercial success, debuting at #1 on the Billboard 200 with 298,000 units moved in its first week, shows Carti still has a strong fanbase, but the critical divide suggests it may be more of a fan favorite than a universal hit. Carti failed to deliver on this project, which is why I am giving this project a light 4.
